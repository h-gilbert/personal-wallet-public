Features

Everything you need to track your wealth

A powerful, self-hosted solution for managing all your financial accounts in one place.

Unified Dashboard

See your complete net worth across all accounts—banks, exchanges, and wallets—in a single view.

Multi-Currency Display

View all your holdings in NZD, USD, or BTC. Switch between currencies with a single click.

Asset Allocation

Visualize your portfolio with interactive donut charts showing allocation by asset type and currency.

Net Worth History

Track your wealth over time with daily snapshots and beautiful interactive line charts.

Transfer Detection

Automatically detect internal transfers between accounts. No more duplicate income/expense entries.

Self-Hosted & Private

Your data stays on your server. No third-party access, no tracking, complete privacy.
